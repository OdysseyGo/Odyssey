About the Project

Imagine exploring a new city, not with a boring guidebook, but by solving a thrilling mystery where the city itself is your game board. Our app transforms city tours into interactive adventures, guiding you through points of interest with captivating stories and puzzles. As you wander through alleys, iconic landmarks, and local gems, the app guides you through a series of captivating stories and immersive challenges that reveal the soul of the city.

We use AI to generate endless unique quests to start, and empower a community of creators to build their own, turning every city street into an unforgettable experience. It’s city exploration, reimagined — making it more accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone.
